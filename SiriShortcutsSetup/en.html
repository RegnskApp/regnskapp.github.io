Siri Shortcuts Setup Guide

Follow these simple steps to set up a Siri Shortcut for logging transactions in BalanceTrackr:

Step 1: Open the Shortcuts app

Launch the built-in Shortcuts app on your iPhone.

Step 2: Tap "Automation" at the bottom

Switch to the Automation tab to create a new shortcut.

Step 3: Tap the plus icon and choose "Transaction"

Tap the + icon at the top right, then select Transaction (with the Wallet symbol).

Step 4: Leave only one card under "When I tap"

Deselect all cards except the one you want to trigger the shortcut from under "When I tap".

Step 5: Confirmation settings

Choose whether you want to receive a confirmation when the automation runs, or let it run silently.

Step 6: Add BalanceTrackr action

Search for "BalanceTrackr" and select whether you want to add a bank account transaction or a credit card transaction.

Step 7: Add variables

Use variables from "Shortcut Input" to fill in Amount and Merchant.
Important: The Account or Credit Card name must match exactly the name used in the BalanceTrackr app.

Step 8: Tap "Done"

You're all set! Tap "Done" to save your new automation.
